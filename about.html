About L.A.S





L.A.S stands for Limmelime Automated Systems. The L.A.S was founded on march 2018, by Limmelime wich is a software developer. This website was made by Limmelime just like all of the other products and content on this site. Limmelime Automated Systems produces all kinds of software. We develope security systems to both our selfs and our users. We also develope tools and add features to our existing tools every day. The tools we develope is making it even easier for us to develope even more. I guess you can say that we work in an efficient way to make our work even more efficient the next day. It's a great technic and our workers are grateful for it. At the moment, we have software engineers, designers, web developers, security specialists, and much more, to make our work fast, efficient and fun. At L.A.S we let our workers work with their own specialists, and if wanted, they can even try out other tasks beside their normal work. By the month of december 2018, we had two workers learning a different developing process than they were used to. In this way, we spred the efficiency to all of our workers. We have a data center full of servers for you to use. You can read more about our dedicated servers and our data center in the servers page.
